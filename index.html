Biography

I'm Anais Lopez Pease—a first-generation Mexican-American woman and a full-stack web developer, proudly graduated from Arizona State University. Growing up in the vibrant San Fernando Valley, Los Angeles, California, I discovered my passion for web development at the age of fourteen. I was an active member of the website club in my high school, igniting a lifelong fascination with crafting digital experiences.

My life took a unique turn when I married a military man. This journey blessed me with the opportunity to immerse myself in diverse cultures, including the stunning landscapes of Wahiawa, Hawaii. While there, I ventured into finance, initially at a bank and later within the higher education sector, accumulating over a decade of valuable experiences.

However, the world-altering arrival of the Covid pandemic prompted profound introspection. Despite cherishing my team and the work I did, I realized a lack of genuine excitement in my professional endeavors.

Driven by a desire for creativity and creation, I made a pivotal decision—to return to academia. I sought a field that would not only fuel my creativity but also empower me to be a creator myself. This led me back to the realm of web development, where I'm driven by the joy of building, designing, and innovating within the digital landscape.

In my free time, I find joy and relaxation in a diverse range of activities. Ceramics is a passion that allows me to shape and mold my creativity into tangible works of art. Immersing myself in the world of literature offers me solace and new perspectives through the pages of countless books.

I'm not just a creator in the digital realm—I'm an avid gamer, exploring virtual worlds and challenging my mind with diverse gameplay. When I step away from screens, I indulge in the serenity of gardening, nurturing life and witnessing the beauty of nature firsthand.

But perhaps my greatest moments of happiness come from spending quality time with my beloved pets and cherished family. Their presence enriches my life, offering moments of pure joy and unwavering support in my journey through both professional and personal endeavors.
